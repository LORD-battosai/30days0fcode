About her

MRS. O.O Kuforiji was born in Gbagada, Lagos,Nigeria.She obtained a national diploma in Business Administration from Yaba College of Technology at age 24.

She worked at UBA bank for 15years before deciding on becoming an educator and later establishing her own school.

MRS. Kuforiji is a hardworking and selfless person with a strong devotion to her family and has a fierce love for her children.

Olubunmi was born on 18 November 1966

She attended Our Lady Of Apostles primary school and CMS grammer school. She obtained her National Diploma from Yabatech College of Technology in 1990.

Established her own school Stars College in 2013
