Timeline

  • MRS. Kuforiji was born on 18th of November 1966 in Gbagada, Lagos.
  • She attended Our Lady Of Apostles primary school and CMS grammer school. She obtained her National Diploma from Yabatech College of Technology in 1990.
  • Her first job was as a cashier at UBA in 1991.
  • She worked at UBA for 15 years. Due to her passion for teaching she became an educator at Chrisland School Organisation in 2006. She became the Asst.Head Teacher but later resigned in 2012 to start up her school
  • She established her school Stars College on 5th of september, 2013
